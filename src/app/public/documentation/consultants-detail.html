To view consultants detail:

1.On the Directory tab click on Consultants.
2.A page with consultants list will appear.
3.To view consultants list by grade, click on the drop down box of grades list then select the grade of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.
4.To view consultants list by type, click on the drop down box of types list then select the type of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.

5.From the list of consultants of your choice click on the name of consultant of your choice, the details will be displayed.
