To add your tender:

1.Click Dashboard tab on the upper right hand side of list of tabs.
2.Click Tenders on the left hand side of dashboard sidebar; a page with list of your tenders will be displayed.


3.Click Add Tender button which is found on MY TENDERS page.


4.Fill the information required and clickPost Tender button which is found on the bottom right.


To view latest tenders:

Click LATEST TENDERS page which is found on Tenders; a page with list of your tenders will be displayed.

To add tender to your choice:

1.Select tender categories of your preferences from Select Tender Categories drop down button.

2.Click Add To Your Choice.
