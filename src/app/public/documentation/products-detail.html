To view products detail:

1.On the Construction tab click on Products.
2.A page with projects list will appear.
3.To view products list by categories, click on the drop down box of companies list then select the companies of your choice from the list that appears and click the search button.
4.To search product fill in the name of product on search products field.
5.From the list of products of your choice on the name of product of your choice, the details will be displayed.
