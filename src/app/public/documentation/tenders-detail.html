To view tenders detail:

1.click Tenders tab.
2.A page with tenders list will appear.
3.To view tenders list by locations, click on the drop down box of locations list then select the field of your choice from the list that appears and click the Search button.
4.To view tenders list category, click on the drop down box of category list then select the type of your choice from the list that appears and click the Search button.


5.From the list of tenders of your choice on the name of tender of your choice, the details will be displayed.


6.To print out tender, click print button.
