To view suppliers detail:

1.On the Directories tab click on Suppliers.
2.A page with suppliers list will appear.
3.To view suppliers list by locations, click on the drop down box of locations list then select the field of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.
4.To view suppliers list by category, click on the drop down box of category list then select the type of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.


5.From the list of suppliers of your choice click on the name of supplier of your choice, the details will be displayed.
