To view projects detail:

1.On the Construction tab click on Projects.
2.A page with projects list will appear.
3.To view projects list by categories, click on the drop down box of companies list then select the companies of your choice from the list that appears and click the search button.
4.To view projects list by companies, click on the drop down box of categories list then select the categories of your choice from the list that appears and click the search button.


5.From the list of projects of your choice click on the name of project of your choice, the details will be displayed.
