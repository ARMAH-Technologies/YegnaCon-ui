To view professionals detail:

1.On the jobs tab click on Suppliers.
2.A page with professionals list will appear.
3.To view professionals list by fields, click on the drop down box of categories list then select the field of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.
4.To view professionals list by experiences, click on the drop down box of companies list then select the type of your choice from the list that appears and click the Submit button.


5.From the list of professionals of your choice click on the name of professional of your choice, the details will be displayed.
