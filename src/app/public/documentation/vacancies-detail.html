To view vacancies detail:

1.On the Jobs tab click on Vacancies.
2.A page with vacancies list will appear.
3.To view vacancies list by fields, click on the drop down box of fields list then select the field of your choice from the list that appears and click the Search button.
4.To view vacancies list by locations, click on the drop down box of locations list then select the type of your choice from the list that appears and click the Search button.


5.From the list of vacancies of your choice click on the name of vacancy of your choice, the details will be displayed.
6.Click print button to print out vacancy.
